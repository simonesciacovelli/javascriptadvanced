"The Utopian" is an experimental website that showcases reputable news from the New York Times' science section in a fictionalized light. The sources are gathered and redistributed with different Sci-Fi illustrations created by Ed Valigursky. The Utopian's goal is to underline how media is evolving and how factual event could be manipulated into something different or altered to reflect a particular narrative. The user is able to click on each article and a brief description will appear, later on, it is possible to access the actual article on the New York Times official website.
